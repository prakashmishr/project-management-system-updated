  • Stage 1:-

    In this stage, Student has to meet their respective mentor(s) to discuss about their project.

  • Stage 2:-

    In this stage, Student has to submit their design, algorithm(s) and some details about technologies which will be used in their project.

  • Stage 3:-

    In this stage, Student has to update their project completion detail on the specified date which will be informed by their respective mentor(s).

    In this stage, Student has to submit their final completion of project to their respective mentor(s) on specified date. Date will be notified by college HODs.
